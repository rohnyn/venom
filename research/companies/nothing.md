# Nothing

## Why it matters

Nothing shows a sharp wedge around memory capture and lightweight delegation without needing a whole new UX model.

## What it appears to be doing

- turning screenshots and voice snippets into durable memory
- building a visible hardware-and-software gesture around capture
- staying close to familiar phone behavior

## Implication

Nothing is a good example of how to introduce operator behavior without forcing users into a new operating metaphor.
