# Open-Source Projects

## Projects to watch

### OpenClaw

The clearest proof that there is massive appetite for open, personal, extensible AI operators.

### Strawberry

Relevant as an Android-native assistant and open-source mobile interaction reference.

### Kai

Relevant for persistent memory, heartbeat, and personal-agent design patterns.

### AnyClaw

Interesting as a direct attempt to package OpenClaw-style behavior on Android.

## Implication

Open-source energy is real, but the deepest activity still appears to be above the OS layer rather than inside AOSP itself.
