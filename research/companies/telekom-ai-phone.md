# Deutsche Telekom AI Phone

## Why it matters

Telekom is testing how agentic mobile behavior can be presented to mainstream customers under a carrier brand.

## What it appears to be doing

- packaging Perplexity-backed AI in a phone product
- moving toward live call assistance and action
- framing AI as something practical rather than experimental

## Implication

This is relevant for the "how does it get to everyone" question more than for open architecture.
