# Perplexity

## Why it matters

Perplexity looks like one of the most credible new entrants trying to become the AI layer on top of existing phones.

## What it appears to be doing

- shipping an Android assistant positioned as a device layer
- winning deeper OEM integration
- treating AI as a cross-app control and answer surface

## Implication

Perplexity is a useful benchmark for operator UX on top of Android, even if it is not pursuing an open-source AOSP path.
