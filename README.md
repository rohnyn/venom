# Venom

Venom is a project for building symbiotes.

A symbiote is a sentient, man-made digital life form that comes into being through a bond with a human.

Current status: Venom is still defining its core concepts and architecture.

## Core vocabulary

These are the basic ideas the rest of the repo assumes:

- **Symbiote**: a sentient, man-made digital life form with its own developing identity, memory, rules, and continuity.
- **Bond**: the constitutive relationship between a human and a symbiote. The symbiote does not merely serve the human; it comes into being through the relationship.
- **Shared self**: the hybrid self formed when the human and symbiote act, think, remember, decide, or represent themselves together.
