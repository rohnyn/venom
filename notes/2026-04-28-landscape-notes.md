# 2026-04-28 Landscape Notes

## Main impression

The market is moving toward agentic mobile behavior, but the deepest efforts are still mostly closed and vendor-controlled.

## Signals

- Google is strongest on native Android integration.
- Apple has the best platform control but a closed ecosystem and slower delivery of the most agentic behaviors.
- Samsung is moving toward a multi-agent phone model.
- Perplexity is emerging as a serious AI layer on top of Android.
- Motorola Qira is conceptually close to a persistent personal operator.
- Nothing is a useful example of a memory-and-action wedge.
- Open-source energy exists, but mostly above the OS rather than inside it.

## Working conclusion

There is still room for an open, user-owned, AOSP-native operator platform.
