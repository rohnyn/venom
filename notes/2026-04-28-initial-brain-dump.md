# 2026-04-28 Initial Brain Dump

- Goal: build the ultimate AI phone as an open-source mobile platform.
- Core ambition: the OpenClaw moment for mobile.
- Desired properties: moddable, extensible, user-controlled, deeply capable, technically serious.
- Important shift: stop thinking of this as an assistant app and start thinking of it as a persistent personal operator.
- Product challenge: preserve the UX users expect while building toward stronger future models.
- Working belief: the OS should own memory, policy, capability brokering, audit, and delegation.
- Open tension: "digital twin" is powerful internally but may be too creepy or overclaimed as external messaging.
